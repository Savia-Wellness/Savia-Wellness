About Me

A coach who believes
wellness is personal

When you change your health, you change your life. I know this firsthand. I found health coaching in the middle of my own turning point, navigating perimenopause and career burnout at the same time. It was the moment I understood that real, lasting change only happens when we look at the whole person, not just one area of life.

This program is for women who are feeling it too: burned out, in transition, or quietly wondering who they are becoming. Whether you're moving through a career shift, a relationship change, or the tender season of an empty nest, this work meets you exactly where you are. Together we explore all the areas that shape your wellbeing: sleep and rest, physical activity, nutrition and lifestyle, your environment, mental and emotional health, and your sense of fulfillment and purpose.

I'm Marcela, completing my NBHWC board certification, the gold standard in health coaching. To sit for the exam, I need to complete 50 supervised client sessions, which means I'm offering a limited number of women the opportunity to receive professional, personalized wellness coaching at no cost. This isn't a trial run. You'll receive real, structured coaching: goal setting, mindset shifts, accountability, and a personalized wellness plan, with the same care and commitment I'll bring to every client going forward.

The Journey

Twelve weeks.
Real transformation.

Each phase builds upon the last, designed to move you from clarity to confidence, one intentional step at a time.

Weeks 1–4

Awareness & Foundation

We begin by understanding where you are, identifying your values, goals, and the patterns shaping your daily life. You'll leave this phase with clarity about what you truly want and why.

Weeks 5–8

Mindful Action & Momentum

From clarity to movement. We'll build practical habits, stress-reduction strategies, and a mindset that supports lasting change, grounded in alignment, not willpower.

Weeks 9–12

Confidence & Integration

We anchor your progress, refine your wellness plan, and set you up for long-term success. You'll finish with a lifestyle that feels genuinely yours, sustainable, joyful, and aligned.
